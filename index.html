While most people don't immediately think of pugs when one mentions "cute dogs", they are actually very underrated in their cuteness. Pugs have the ability to simultaneously look old and young at the same time - sort of like a baby Yoda. They are mysterious creatures as you can never really tell if they are concerned about something or simply living their best life. Ahhh pugs.
